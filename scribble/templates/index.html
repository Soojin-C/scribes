Scribble


How to Play

Scribble is a multiplayer drawing game where players take turns drawing and guessing the chosen word.
If it is your turn to draw, choose one of the three words and draw in on the canvas.
If you are not drawing, type your guess in chat.
As you earn points, it will be displayed on the scoreboard.
